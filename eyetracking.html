Key Findings

Heatmap Analysis

The heatmap visualization revealed distinct patterns in participant attention:

  • Yellow and red zones indicated high fixation areas
  • Blue and green tones showed areas of lower attention
  • Clear correlation between legend changes and attention patterns

Emotional Response Data

Analysis of emotional fluctuations revealed:

  • Tracked "happy" and "surprise" emotional states
  • K-coefficient peaks indicating heightened engagement
  • Correlation between visual elements and emotional responses

Research Impact

The eye-tracking survey provided valuable insights into participant attention and emotional responses to the "Arctic Sea Ice Decline" map. The use of heatmaps and Areas of Interest (AOIs) revealed key areas of visual focus, while emotional analysis added depth to understanding viewer interactions. The high-quality eye-tracking data ensures the credibility of findings and supports further exploration into visual cognitive processes.
