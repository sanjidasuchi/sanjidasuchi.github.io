Interactive Map of Sierra Nevada Climbing Routes

Explore snow and ice climbing routes in the Sierra Nevada Mountains through this interactive web app. The map features filtering capabilities by route type, allowing climbers to visualize and prepare for their preferred climbing experiences. Using ArcGIS Instant Apps with Interactive Legend template, users can easily navigate and discover routes that match their interests and skill levels. The app also offers a 3D viewing option to explore routes in a three-dimensional environment.
